About The Project

This GCC Field Force and Doctor’s Engagement Dashboard provides a concise overview of healthcare engagement and sales performance across the GCC region from July 2024 to June 2025. It highlights key metrics such as 1,000 total doctors engaged, 5,000 prescriptions generated, 4,000 visits conducted by 6 field representatives, and a total sales value of $15 million. The dashboard shows that Dermatologists, Oncologists, and Orthopedic specialists account for the highest prescription volumes, while NeuroMax leads in product sales at $4.5M. Qatar ranks highest in RX conversion rate at 135%, followed by UAE and Kuwait. Additionally, Dr. Khalid Al-Farsi and Dr. Ali Al-Kuwaiti are among the most visited doctors. The dashboard also tracks sales and visit trends over time, helping stakeholders assess field productivity, doctor engagement, and overall market impact.
